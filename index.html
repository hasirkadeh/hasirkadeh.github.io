About Me

I am a Bohemian (Boho) designer with a passion for blending creativity, art, and nature to transform spaces into soulful and free-spirited environments. With a deep understanding of Boho aesthetics, I craft spaces that are rich in textures, patterns, and colors—always ensuring a harmonious balance between function and beauty. My design philosophy is rooted in the belief that each space should tell a unique story, reflecting the individuality of the people who live or work in it. From concept to execution, I focus on creating tranquil, warm, and inviting spaces that embrace the essence of freedom and comfort.

What I’m good at?

Interior Styling & Decorating

I specialize in creating cozy, eclectic spaces that combine vintage pieces with modern comforts, layering patterns, and textures for an authentic Boho feel.

Sustainable Design

I believe in using natural, eco-friendly materials and upcycled furniture to create spaces that are both beautiful and environmentally responsible.

Space Planning

My approach to space planning focuses on openness and flexibility, allowing for multifunctional areas that feel airy and comfortable.

Personalized Touch

Every design I create reflects the personality and lifestyle of my clients. From vibrant color palettes to earthy tones, I tailor each project to meet your unique needs.

My Work

My portfolio showcases a range of eclectic and beautifully styled spaces that embody the free-spirited nature of Boho design. Each project is a testament to my belief in the power of creativity and individuality. Whether it’s a cozy living room filled with vibrant textiles or a minimalist space with a natural palette, my work reflects a balance between aesthetics and practicality. I invite you to explore my projects and see how I bring spaces to life with warmth, comfort, and a touch of artistic charm.
